Eren Jaëger

The man who saved Eldia.

Here's a timeline of Eren Jaëger's life:

  • 835 - Born in Shinganshina District of Wall Maria

  • 844 - Eren saves Mikasa from human traffickers and accepts her into his family

  • 845 - Eren's mother dies and he swears to destroy all the titans.

  • 847 - Eren and his two childhood friend, Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert join the training corps.

  • 850 - Graduates ranking 5th and then finds out he can turn into a titan. The scouts arranged that he be enlisted as a fellow scout for monitoring sake and to put his abilities to good use.

  • 850 - Along with the rest of the survey corps Eren captures the female titan.

  • 850 - Eren along with the rest of the scouts fight the corrupt government and make the reightfull heir to the throne Historia Reiss Queen.

  • 850 - Eren and the Survey Corps fight the armored, beast and collosal titans where they manage to dispatch the collosal titan and get Armin Arlert to inherit the collosal titan's powers. They also manage to drive back the armored and beast titans while sacrificing most of the survey corps in the process.

  • 854 - Eren fights Marly who declared war on Eldia at the world council.

  • 854 - Eren managed to start the rumbling wiping out 80% of humanity outside of the walls while leaving as many people alive as he could. Eren is killed by his lover Mikasa so that they can be branded as heroes and the so that the war can end. Therefore saving Eldia and as many people as he possibly could who lived on other continents.
